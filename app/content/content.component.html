Post-Impressionsm Art

Post-Impressionism is an art movement that developed in
France between 1886 and 1905. It is a reaction to the Impressionist movement's
focus on the objective depiction of light and color.



Expressionism Art

Expressionism is an art movement that began
in Germany in the early 20th century, It tends to be emotional and sometimes mystical.
It can be seen as an extension of Romanticism.
